What We Are

Nudge is a not-for-profit mentorship program dedicated to supporting students from underrepresented and/or underprivileged backgrounds, including but not limited to first-generation and low-income students. 

What We Do

We aim to reduce the resource gap in international education systems by matching mentors with backgrounds from overseas universities and high school mentees who actively seek and need assistance in exploring programs beyond the traditional Gaokao system.
