We aim to reduce the resource gap in international education systems by matching mentors with backgrounds from overseas universities and high school mentees who actively seek assistance in exploring programs beyond the traditional Gaokao system. 

Founder’s Statement

A lot of people say that this is the truth of volunteer work: When we’re helping others, we’re also helping ourselves.

I don’t particularly like this statement.

When we are helping others, we are not helping ourselves.

They are the ones helping us.

They are the ones generous enough to share their stories with complete strangers, and to trust our assistance on their personal concerns.


They help us see beyond what we are looking for. 

"Thus, Nudge was founded in the hope of passing on the kindness, knowledge, and wisdom that my schools, professors, scholarship donors, and others around me have generously gifted me."
